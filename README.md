# Vaibhav Global Trade & Language Consultancy

## Industrial Consultancy, Multilingual Translation & Global Trade Platform

A professional, SEO-optimized multi-page website built to generate qualified industrial leads for multilingual translation, Chinese coordination, equipment sourcing, manpower supply, and visa consultancy services.

This platform is designed for credibility, authority positioning, and high-intent conversions across heavy industry and government-linked sectors.

# üöÄ Key Features

## Centralized Contact Configuration

All phone numbers, WhatsApp links, and email addresses are managed from:

src/config/contact.ts

This ensures:

- Single source of truth  
- Easy maintenance  
- No hardcoded duplication  

---

## WhatsApp Lead Automation

Form submission dynamically generates:
https://wa.me/
<number>?text=<encoded_message>


- No backend required  
- Instant lead capture through WhatsApp
